Localization

Creating web experiences that are truly international is a challenge. Calcite Web aims to take care of as much of the busywork as possible.

Right To Left

Some languages such as Arabic and Hebrew read from right to left instead of left to right as English does. This means that for those languages all spacing and layout (columns, floats, padding, margin) must be flipped horizontally.

In order to have your document render from right to left, simply set the dir attribute:

<html lang="ar" dir="rtl">

Once this has been added, Calcite Web will automatically flip around all styles for you.

Internationalization of Type

Most languages will be rendered in the main brand typeface, Avenir Next. Some languages, however, require characters that are not available in Avenir Next. In those situations Calcite Web will automatically use a variety of international typefaces which have been selected to match the modern look and feel of Avenir. These typefaces are outlined below:
